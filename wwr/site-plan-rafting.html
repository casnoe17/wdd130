Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of this website is to provide users with a single location where they can learn about the sport of white water rafting, the services our company offers, and how to reserve rafting excursions. By doing so it will help us reach a wider audience and build revenue.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target audience will be people between the ages of 18 and 35. The website will target both new and experienced rafters. What questions will be answered by them visiting the site? The site will provide users primarily with information on what the sport of white water rafting is, our excursions, and how to reserve an excursion. How will my audience primarily access my site? The target audience will primarily access the site via laptop or mobile devices.
